How we choose our picks

We don’t test products in a lab, so we rely on the next best thing: a deep dive into long-term owner feedback across hundreds of verified reviews and community forums. We look for patterns – whether a powder clumps, causes aftertaste, or stops dissolving after a few weeks. We also track how brands handle complaints, ingredient sourcing, and third-party testing claims, because trust matters more than a slick website. We cross-reference that with published expert reviews from registered dietitians and consumer-testing labs (like the ones run by consumer advocacy groups). The real differentiators are what actually breaks: a fine grind that won’t dissolve, a cheap tub that spills, or a “neutral” flavor that still tastes fishy. Our picks are the ones that consistently avoid those pitfalls and have the reputation to back it up.
